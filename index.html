Welcome


Welcome to We Believe! The page is dedicated to providing users with an introduction to the Catholic Church and basic Catholic Teachings. We Believe is intended to target those with little to no background in Catholicism, however it can be used for people from all spiritual backgrounds and levels of theology! Simply browse the menu bar for topics of interest. The information is broken into a few groups:

Beginnerâ€™s Guide: Meant to mimic a church RCIA program, this section explains the totality of the faith at an introductory level, with several visual aides to help learn and develop your understanding of the Faith.

Catechesis: Designed for those who have already been introduced to the faith, but want to learn more. This section goes more into detail on the topics provided, and is intended to build off an introductory level of knowledge.

Youth: A collection of topics meant for children and teenagers. Meant to provide them with introduction to some topics that can lay a spiritual foundation as they begin their lives.

Church History: A background of the Church from its inception to present day

Resources: A list of resources that are available that supplement this knowledge, key resources such as the Bible and the Catechism, as well as many other good references to learn about the faith.

The column on the far left has a few more easy access links. The creators of this page hope it helps you grow closer to God through an introductory approach to Catechesis. Thanks and God bless!
